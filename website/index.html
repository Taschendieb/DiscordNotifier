Discord Notifier allows you to ask who is online on any of your Discord Servers, with online meaning the user is connected to a voice channel.
Note: Only Users on Servers the Discord Notifier Bot is a member of are reported back.

  • 1. Add the "Discord Notifier" Bot to your server
  • 2. Download the Alexa Skill or simply use "Ok Google"
  • 3. Have Fun!

Invocation: "Ok Google / Alexa, ask Discord Notifier who is online"


Important: We are not associated with Discord in any way and simply provide this service using their official API
